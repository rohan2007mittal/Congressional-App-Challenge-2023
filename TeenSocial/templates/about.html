RecoverTogether:
Empowerment Through Connection

We're dedicated to facilitating transformation for people tackling addiction and mental health challenges. We have a firm belief in the profound impact of social factors on recovery. A 2014 study published in the National Library of Medicine was conducted with emerging adults (aged 18-24) dealing with substance use disorders. A key finding from this study was the evolution of a social network: through treatment combined with a social setting, participants retained and formed new relations with low-risk friends, all while decreasing more high-risk connections. This dynamic signifies the potential for positive change within social networks, which is central to our mission.
